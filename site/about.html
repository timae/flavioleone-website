about

Flavio Leone is a photographer and visual artist based in Switzerland.

He began his photographic career at the age of sixteen when he became involved in the Swiss skateboarding scene. After completing an apprenticeship as a print technologist, he transitioned into the role of a photographer's assistant, gaining experience in both commercial and editorial photography. His artistic path has now led him to combine portraiture and various forms of physical movement with his interest in abstract photography.

For commissions, prints and collaborations, see the contact page.
